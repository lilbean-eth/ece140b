Key Value Proposition
Clean Sneaks is an appliance that makes our users lives easier by maintaining their favorite pairs of shoes
Before, those people who enjoy having a clean aesthetic would need to maintain their shoes themselves or even worse, BUY new ones :/

They would have maintained their shoes using traditional maintenance methods which includes a tooth brush, some soap, time in the sun, and don't forget, SOMEONE'S PRECIOUS TIME!

Another method would be throwing them in the washer machine, which has the possibility of ruining both the shoes and the machine! :///

With Clean Sneaks, our users can just DROP THEM IN AND FORGET ABOUT THEM! :)
