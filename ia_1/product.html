Clean Sneaks Automatic Shoe Cleaner
Who is it for?

Clean Sneaks is an automatic shoe cleaner that is meant for anyone who hates having to spend time maintaining their favorite pair of shoes!
What is it?

The Clean Sneaks machine is approximately the size of a carry-on luggage bag, and is meant to maintain the cleanliness of your favorite pair of shoes.
What does it do?

Ranges of use could be from everyday wear or from more strenuous applications, and the machine can be used as often as needed. Whether that be every couple of days, or every couple of weeks.
How does it do it?

Depending on the shoe and material, our machine works by brushing off dirt using dry brushes and uses cleaning solution and wet brushes to clean off more stubborn grime.
Key Benefits?

Keeping your shoes presentable!
Extending the life of your favorite pair of shoes!
Saving your precious time!
